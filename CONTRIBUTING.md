# Contributing to RavLight

Thanks for your interest in contributing to RavLight Core 🚀

---

## 🧠 Before you contribute

This project uses a **dual licensing model**:
- AGPLv3 (open source)
- Commercial licensing

To make this sustainable, contributions must follow specific rules.

---

## 📜 Contribution License Agreement (CLA)

By submitting a pull request, you agree that:

1. Your contribution is licensed under **AGPLv3**
2. You grant the project owner the right to:
   - use your contribution in commercial licensing
   - relicense your contribution if needed

This ensures:
- the project remains open-source
- the project can evolve commercially

---

## ❗ Important

If you are not comfortable with these terms, please do not submit contributions.

---

## 🛠 Guidelines

- One feature per PR
- Keep modules isolated (`MODULE_*`, `FIXTURE_*`)
- Avoid cross-dependencies
- Follow existing code style

---

## ❤️ Thanks

Even small contributions matter.
